# THIRTY-ONE

A roguelike card game inspired by the classic card game 31.

## Play Online

Visit the GitHub Pages deployment to play directly in your browser.

## Features

- **9 Unique Decks** - Standard, Vampire, Gambler, Alchemist, Phoenix, Merchant, Wild, Glass, Blessed
- **8 Ascension Levels** - Increasing difficulty with stacking penalties
- **32 Modifiers** - Value, Suit, Threshold, Action, Hand, Entropy, and Synergy categories
- **12 Relics** - Powerful passive abilities unlocked through achievements
- **8 Spirits** - Companions with passive bonuses, loyalty rewards, and abandonment conditions
- **5 Card Marks** - Flame, Ice, Crown, Echo, Debt
- **18 Bosses** - Unique rule-breaking challenges
- **7 Challenges** - Special game modes
- **Daily & Endless Modes**

## Spirits

Spirits are companions you can buy at the shop. Each has:
- **Passive**: Always active bonus
- **Loyalty**: Reward at 5 loyalty points
- **Abandonment**: Condition that makes them leave
